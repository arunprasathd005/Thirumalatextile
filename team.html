Our facilities

PROCESSING

The Fabric is Processed as per the Requirement of Customers by using Both Indigenous and Premium Imported Machineries.

  • Singeing machine with Desizing facility,Continues Bleaching Range and Drying Range.
  • Stenter of chamber with pin & clip Rotary STORMAC RD VI GOLD & LAKSHMI with 12 Colours,Loopager machine and Slit open machine for knit fabrics.
  • Batching machine for tubular knit fabrics,Zero Zero machine for woven fabrics and The maximum width of printing and processing can be under taken up to 72 inch width.
  • Maximum no of colours can be printed 12 colours,The printing repeat size can be 640, 720, 820, 914, and 1018 and All type of Mill Finishing.

DEPARTMENTS

Designing Department

Print designers specialize in the creation of visual designs for printing.They also ensure that the dimensions and technical specifications are suitable for printing.Print designers work, often in a digital format, to conceptualize and design an end product that’s usually printed in physical form.Our designing artist able to complete and submit strike off with 3 days.

Strike off Department

The specimen or swatch submitted to buyer for print colors approval, For strike-off sample printing, actual production fabric and actual base color is used.Once design work completed we able submit strike off within day.
