Our Values

As a company policy, we believe in living and practicing internationally approved business ethics and norms that distinguish Globe from its counterparts. Values – honesty, integrity and human dignity, besides ethical approach in engagements with all connected stakeholders; customers, suppliers, employees and others, remain close to our hearts resulting in our hard earned repute and success.

Our Mission

Tirumala printing mill is committed to the consistent upliftment and improvement of both its products and its responsibilities towards it’s employees. Striving hard to achieve highest levels of customer satisfaction through timely deliveries along with enhanced quality and value of all our products.

Our Vision

At Tirumala printing mill with well-defined quality objectives in mind to attain customer satisfaction, is at the heart of our business policy and strategy. We will always endeavor to satisfy our customers’ needs and exceed expectations through win-win relationships and delight them. Our vision is to serve our customers with dedication and offer specialized services with GLOBE quality assurance, walking the ‘extra mile.’
